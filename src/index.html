What We Do

Blackbird is a student-driven initiative building thoughtful products for both the USC and Los Angeles communities. We are a nimble and diverse team composed of the best developers, designers, project managers, and marketers at USC. We care deeply about building beautiful, impactful experiences and take pride in doing so at the highest level.
